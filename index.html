How this game works

1
You are the Governor of Texas. Your term began the day you took office in this app. The state's rise to #1 in governance depends on one thing: you holding the line, one day at a time.
2
Log once a day. A clean day establishes the next county on the map — Texas built 254 of them between 1836 and 1921, and you'll found every one in true historical order, each with its own story.
3
Streaks earn points. Milestones pay out at 7, 14, 30, 45, 60 and 90 days (and every further 90 after that). A setback costs 3 points and resets the streak — but it never takes a county away. Ground gained is ground kept.
4
Day 254 is victory. When the last county, Kenedy, joins the map, the game ends: Texas is ranked #1 in the nation for governance, and the credit is yours.

A word from your Chief of Staff

Governor — the point of this ledger isn't perfection. It's direction. Urges pass, usually within 20 minutes; a county on the map is permanent. If a setback happens, log it honestly, take the 3-point hit, and show up tomorrow. No governor in history kept a perfect record, and the good ones are remembered for what they built, not the days they stumbled.

Practical field tactics: when an urge hits, leave the room, drink water, do 20 push-ups, or step outside — change the scenery and the moment usually breaks. Keep devices out of the bedroom, fill evenings with real plans, and tell yourself only this: not today. You never have to win forever. Just today. And if this habit feels bigger than willpower, talking to a counselor or a trusted friend is a strong move, not a weak one. Even governors have advisors.
